Brilliant Pharmaceutical Co. Ltd

We are committed to offer high quality and affordable medicines, medical equipment and other healthcare products to ensure that all South Sudanese and the people of this region gain access to quality healthcare at an affordable cost.

Our Mission  
 Brilliant Pharmaceutical Co. Ltd strives to provide excellent products and services to the clients by being honest, fair, reliable and responsible in all our business practices. Through our knowledge and significant experience in the industry, we add value to our customers and build long term relationships which are mutually beneficial.

Our Vision  
 To be one of the leading & trusted pharmaceutical companies in the region by offering time-demanded, quality products & services that improves the quality of lives of millions of people.

Core Values  
 Integrity All our business decisions are guided by the virtue of integrity. To us, integrity means doing the right thing always. We are committed to the highest ethical standard in all our business operations.

Our Management  
 Our Management team consists of highly-qualified and high-performing professionals with rich experience in the pharmaceutical industry as well as in other business fields. These individuals bring in passion and exceptional expertise to determine the focus and strategic direction of our company.

Our Partners  
 Brilliant Pharmaceutical Co. Ltd Is committed to offer high quality and affordable medicines to the people of South Sudan and the region at large. So, we are partnering with the reputed manufacturers of high quality Pharmaceutical products from various countries.

Our Goals  

  • To Create a distinctive, highly Trained Workforce.
  • To maintain and enhance cooperative relationship with Physicians & health professionals to better serve the healthcare needs of our community.
  • To treat our customers and partners with respect and dignity.
  • To Develop a loyal and productive team.
  • To Build skill-based assets for increased competitive advantage.
  • To Increase profitability through superior service.
  • To provide High Quality Medical Services.
  • To provide Good Quality & affordable medicines
