Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?
Our pharmacy is open Monday through Sunday 8AM to 12AM.
Do you accept my insurance?
We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card to verify coverage.
How can I transfer my prescription from another pharmacy?
You can transfer your prescription by providing us with the details of your previous pharmacy, including the prescription number. We’ll handle the rest!
What should I do if I miss a dose of my medication?
If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. If it’s almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one. Never double up!
Can I refill my prescription online?
Yes, you can refill your prescription through our website contact info. You’ll need your prescription number and last name.
How long will it take to fill my prescription?
Most prescriptions are filled within 15-30 minutes. If you call ahead, we can prepare it for you in advance.
Can you explain the side effects of my medication?
Yes, we can provide you with a medication guide that outlines common side effects, and you can also consult with our pharmacists.
What do I do if I have questions about my prescription?
You can ask any of our pharmacists directly during your visit or contact us via phone or email for assistance.
What should I do if I have an allergic reaction to a medication?
If you experience a severe allergic reaction, seek immediate medical attention. For mild reactions, contact us for guidance.
